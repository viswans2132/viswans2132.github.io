Biography of Demetris Zeinalipour

I am a graduate research student from Robotics Research Center at International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad with research experience in adaptive controllers for Euler-Lagarangian systems. My research is towards complete autonomy of payload carrying aerial robots. On this pursuit, I have proposed and implemented adaptive controllers for drones to tackle uncertainties in modelling the dynamics and external disturbances. I am looking for opportunities for doctoral research in the fields of state constrained adaptive controller and control-aware trajectory planning.
