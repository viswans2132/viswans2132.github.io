I am a graduate research student from Robotics Research Center at International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad with research experience in adaptive controllers for Euler-Lagarangian systems. My research is towards complete autonomy of payload carrying aerial robots. On this pursuit, I have proposed and implemented adaptive controllers for drones to tackle uncertainties in modelling the dynamics and external disturbances. Projects

  • Dynamic aerial payload delivery: An aerial robot carrying a package follows a mobile ground robot autonomously and delivers the package on the ground robot while it is running.
  • Elasticopter: A novel shape-changing quadrotor mechanism and its controller are designed for carrying payloads of different sizes and masses efficiently.
  • Obstacle avoidance for aerial robot: An environment is mapped using OctoMap with the help of visual-inertial sensors and obstacles are avoided by generating an RRT* based trajectory, which is further smoothened by fitting a minimum snap trajectory over it.
  • Biped Locomotion: A 3-feet Biped is designed and its kinematics is modelled to create a trajectory for its locomotion.
  • Writing Robot: A 3-DoF arm is designed to write the letters spoken to it through speech input.
